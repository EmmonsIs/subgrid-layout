Things In jars

People put things in jars for many reasons:

  • Storage
  • Concoting
  • For the funny

However!

People will often put things in jars for nefarious reasons not listed above.

The Jars

People will put figures of characters from anime and cartoons into jars for a reason other than storage.

The Liquid

These disgracful people will often fill these jars that contain these figures with a slime like substance giving the figure a more grotesque look.
